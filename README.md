# ringo-docs

**Intro**

As part of the smart phone experience, Ringo allows you and your team to easily collaborate on customer requests making your service more smooth and efficient. In this guide we will walk you through how to invite your team to join your workspace, how to chat in internal thread and how to configure you workspace in the besy wat for you and your team.

**How it works**

- Your team will share the same inbox where all customer texts and calls go.
- Team members can reply and answer to customer as per their availability
- Team members can tag each other internally on customer messages to better solve inquiries

**Adding Team Members to Workspace**

As a business owner you will set up your Ringo account (registers your business with 10DLC, etc), once that is done it’s time to add your team members so you can collaborate on the same workspace.

- Go to Settings
- Click on “Manage Team & Invitations”
- Click on “+”
- Enter the person’s email address (registered on Ringo with their phone number)
- Choose Role:
  - **Admins** have full access, including user management and workspace settings.
  - **Members** can access inboxes, contacts, and chats, but have limited administrative privileges.

Invited members will appear on the “Invited” tab, and once they have received your invite via email and accepted it they will show as “Active”

**Changing Team Roles**

If you want to change someone’s role:

- Go to Settings
- Click on “Manage Teams & Invitations”
- Check Active members
- “Edit Role” and pick between Member or Admin.

**Accepting a Workspace Invite**

As a team member your Admin will invite you to join their workspace.

- Go to your email
- Accept the invite
- Go through the onboarding process, create your account and get your new Ringo number

**Collaborative Chats**

Once your team is all connected in the same workspace, you can start to collaborate on customer chats.

Internal Threads:

- Go to customer chat
- Click on specific message to check which team member sent it
- Long press on specific message you want to leave a comment on
- Click on “Open Internal Thread”
- Leave a comment and/ or tag team member by typing “@” followed by team member’s name
- Attach video, photos or documents if needed
